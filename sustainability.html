Tomorrow drives Aldair

Since we invented the automobile 13 years ago, Aldair has always been driven by tomorrow
to develop and enhance individual mobility. With our pioneering spirit,
we are on track to drive the transformation towards a more sustainable future.

Step by step we are striving for progress: We are about to open an innovative battery recycling factory
the first of its kind in Africa. And in 2025, we will launch our ‚electric-first' platform AMA (Aldair Modular Architecture),
marking the next step into an electric future by offering a fully electric alternative for each model.

Sustainability goes hand in hand with responsibility...

The topic of sustainability is often associated with climate and the environment, which we address through innovation and development.
For us, sustainability is more of a holistic way to think about our company, our operations,
our employees and our partners – looking at all ESG (Environment, Social & Governance) dimensions.
